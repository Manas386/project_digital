### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
I designed an infographic using Canva to explain:
- What digital literacy means (smart use of technology, finding information responsibly, staying safe online).

The biggest lesson was that keeping designs minimal makes information easier to read.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness: UPI Fraud
Researched UPI payment fraud, where scammers trick victims into entering their PIN on a collect request (which actually sends money).  
Key takeaways:
- Even tech‑savvy people can fall for scams.  
- I stopped using public Wi‑Fi for financial transactions.  
- Learned about the **National Cyber Crime Portal** (cybercrime.gov.in) and helpline **1930**.
