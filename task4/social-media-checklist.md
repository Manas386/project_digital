# 📌 Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use as a College Student

## ✅Do's

1.**Think before you post — once it’s online, it stays there.** 

2.**Keep your profile clean and respectful**

3.**Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings.**

4.**Share useful, positive, or meaningful content.** 

5.**Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.** 

---

## ❌Don'ts

1.**Don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.**

2.**Don’t post anything offensive, abusive, or inappropriate.** 

3.**Don’t forward fake news without checking facts.**

4.**Don’t spend too much time scrolling and ignore studies.** 

5.**Don’t engage in online fights or arguments.**
